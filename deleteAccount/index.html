Delete Account Request - Cario

If you wish to delete your Cario account and all associated personal data, please follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to asaqarbeh@gmail.com with the subject line "Delete My Account".
  2. Include your registered email address or username associated with your account.
  3. We will verify your identity and process your request within 7 business days.

Data Deletion Details

Upon confirmation, we will delete all your personal information including profile data, usage history, and preferences from our servers.

Some anonymized and aggregated data may be retained for analytics and fraud prevention purposes for up to 30 days.

If you have any questions or concerns, feel free to contact us at asaqarbeh@gmail.com.
